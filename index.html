Looking for a full-stack developer?

Well, you just might have found yourself one! Please read on to learn a little bit more about me...

I am an experienced language professional seeking work in the field of web development. After spending several years teaching and translating Russian and Polish, in early 2015 I decided to embark on a new challenge and started to teach myself “21st-century languages,” including Ruby, JavaScript and jQuery, SQL, HTML, and CSS. I am currently a student in Dev Bootcamp Chicago’s 19-week intensive program, where I am deepening my knowledge of all aspects of full-stack web development.

I love creating things that are well-designed, both in terms of organization and functionality as well as usability and aesthetics. Check out my projects page for glimpses at some of the recent projects I’ve worked on.

For more information about me and my background, please see my bio page and résumé. I also maintain a blog, which contains weekly posts on technical topics and other assorted thoughts.
